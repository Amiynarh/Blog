ABOUT US

Amina Lawal

Founder

Welcome to MentorToPerfection, my name is Amina Lawal, and I’m the founder of MentorToPerfection, a place to find not only wholesome and simple motivation, but encouragement, inspiration, and motivation for your journey to get fit for life. At MentorToPerfection, it is truly my greatest heart’s desire to help others find encouragement and fulfillment through a balance of healthy living principals for life.

I'm a Nigerian, a web designer, writer, blogger, humanitarian, social activist, motivational speaker and a people’s person! At least that’s what I tell myself. I’m currently undergoing a Software Engineering degree in Bayero University Kano.

I try to guide others into believing in themselves and help gain their self-esteem, I believe life is too short to spend simply grinding away through motions feeling unfulfilled. I want you to have a full life that you are happy and content with while finding the courage to pursue your dreams. Consider me your online friend in personal development and growth.
